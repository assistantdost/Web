Virtual Assistant

Your reliable virtual assistant designed to simplify your daily tasks and enhance your productivity on the Windows platform.

Powered by smart algorithms, DOST possesses an exceptional ability to understand natural language commands.

With DOST by your side, you can navigate through your Windows effortlessly, streamlining your workflow and saving  valuable time.

Voice Recognition

DOST is equipped with cutting-edge voice recognition technology, enabling you to control your computer using voice commands.

Simply speak your requests, and DOST will carry them out promptly, from launching applications to searching the web or even adjusting system settings.

Additionally, DOST also supports call by name functionality. You can assign a custom name to your virtual assistant, allowing you to address it directly for initiating commands or seeking assistance.

Multiple Tasks

DOST, your versatile virtual assistant, can handle various tasks effortlessly!

You can simply ask DOST to play a song or artist, and it will fetch music from YouTube, adjusting the volume as per your preference.

Stay updated on current events, sports, weather, and more by asking DOST to fetch local news based on your location or a specific region of interest.

Personalization

DOST offers a range of personalization options to make your virtual assistant experience more tailored and enjoyable.

You can choose a unique name for your virtual assistant, allowing you to address it with a name that resonates with you personally.

Choose between a male or female voice for DOST, allowing you to interact with a virtual assistant that aligns with your preference.

Customize the appearance of DOST by selecting from various themes and visual styles. Choose color schemes, backgrounds, and visual elements that suit your taste, creating a visually pleasing interface.

GPT 3.5 Turbo Assisted

DOST is equipped with an advanced answer command powered by GPT-3.5 Turbo, which enhances its ability to provide accurate and comprehensive responses to a wide range of questions and queries.

This can tap into vast amounts of knowledge and generate human-like responses, enabling it to assist you with in-depth information, explanations, and insights on various topics.

Whether you need assistance with general knowledge, specific subjects, or complex inquiries, DOST's answer command can deliver intelligent and informative responses.
